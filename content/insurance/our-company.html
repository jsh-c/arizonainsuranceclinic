About us

WELCOME TO ARIZONA INSURANCE CLINIC

We are an independent insurance agency that has served Pima County, Arizona and surrounding counties since 1983. Whether you need insurance for your your home, your car, your rental properties or your business, you can feel confident that you have found an insurance agency with proven insurance professionals to take care of all of your insurance needs.
We specialize in low cost, quality Arizona auto and homeowners insurance coverage. Our sophisticated computers shop many insurance companies to provide you with the auto and home insurance policy coverage you want, at the lowest possible price in Arizona.
The Tucson, AZ area has special insurance concerns that drive the cost of insurance. We work to provide you with solutions to the rising cost of all types of insurance in Tucson and throughout the Grand Canyon State. Since we are a full-service agency, we offer low cost solutions for Arizona Auto and Homeowners insurance, as well as Flood, Motorcycle, Boat, RV, Umbrella, and Renters insurance.
Arizona Insurance Clinic also offers coverage's for your Arizona business insurance needs. Whether it's General Liability, Commercial Property, Commercial Auto or Workers Comp insurance coverage, we can design a competitive, affordable insurance package that is tailored to your needs. As an independent full-service insurance agency, we can also assist your Tucson or Phoenix area business with Arizona Builders Risk insurance and other commercial insurance services.
Whether you're a contractor, builder, large employer, small business owner, or a new start-up company looking for affordable insurance plan coverage, we can fulfill your Pima County or Maricopa County, AZ and statewide commercial insurance needs. Fill out a secure online quote request form and in a timely manner receive a confidential and thorough business insurance quote, or contact us by phone at (520) 323-6887 to speak with one of our independent Arizona commercial insurance professionals.
